<text>
### <idea>
**Design Movement**: **Corporate Modernism (Swiss Style)**
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Clarity & Precision**: Data is the hero. Every pixel serves a purpose. No decorative fluff.
2.  **Grid-Based Order**: A strict, visible or implied grid system organizes complex information into digestible chunks.
3.  **Hierarchy through Scale**: Use dramatic size differences in typography to guide the eye, rather than color.
4.  **Objective Neutrality**: The interface feels like a high-end instrumentâ€”reliable, unbiased, and professional.

**Color Philosophy**:
*   **Deep Navy Blue (#0F172A)**: Represents trust, authority, and stability. Used for primary navigation and key actions.
*   **Cool Gray (#F8FAFC)**: A clean, sterile background that allows data to pop.
*   **Electric Blue (#3B82F6)**: Used sparingly for interactive elements and active states, signifying digital precision.
*   **Alert Colors**: Muted reds and oranges for status updates, keeping the overall tone calm.
*   *Intent*: To foster a sense of calm control and professional efficiency.

**Layout Paradigm**:
*   **Modular Dashboard**: A bento-box style layout where each widget is a self-contained card.
*   **Sidebar Navigation**: A persistent, collapsible sidebar for quick access to different modules.
*   **Data-First Tables**: High-density tables with sticky headers and clear sorting indicators.

**Signature Elements**:
1.  **Card-Based Architecture**: Everything lives in a card with a subtle border and no shadow (flat design).
2.  **Monospace Data**: Use a monospaced font for all numerical data to ensure alignment and readability.
3.  **Status Pills**: Small, rounded capsules for status indicators (e.g., "Pending", "Completed").

**Interaction Philosophy**:
*   **Instant Feedback**: Hover states are crisp and immediate.
*   **Keyboard Efficiency**: Power users can navigate via keyboard shortcuts.
*   **Drill-Down**: Clicking a summary card reveals detailed data without a full page reload.

**Animation**:
*   **Micro-interactions**: Subtle color shifts on hover.
*   **Data Loading**: Skeleton screens instead of spinners to maintain layout stability.
*   **Transitions**: Fast (150ms) fades for content updates.

**Typography System**:
*   **Headings**: **Inter** (Bold/ExtraBold) - For clear, authoritative section titles.
*   **Body**: **Inter** (Regular) - For high legibility.
*   **Data**: **JetBrains Mono** or **Roboto Mono** - For numbers and IDs.
</idea>

### <idea>
**Design Movement**: **Glassmorphism (Frosted Tech)**
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Depth & Layering**: Using translucency and blur to create a sense of hierarchy and context.
2.  **Light & Shadow**: Soft, diffused shadows and highlights to mimic physical materials.
3.  **Vibrant Accents**: Using bright, saturated colors against the frosted glass to create visual interest.
4.  **Fluidity**: The interface feels organic and smooth, like a modern app ecosystem.

**Color Philosophy**:
*   **Ocean Blue Gradient**: A background that shifts from deep blue to teal, visible through the glass layers.
*   **Frosted White (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.7))**: The primary container material.
*   **Neon Blue (#00F0FF)**: For high-priority alerts and active states.
*   **Deep Indigo Text**: For readability against the light glass.
*   *Intent*: To make the CRM feel cutting-edge, innovative, and pleasant to use daily.

**Layout Paradigm**:
*   **Floating Panels**: Content areas "float" above the background.
*   **Centralized Focus**: Key metrics are centered and elevated.
*   **Overlay Modals**: Details open in glass overlays rather than new pages.

**Signature Elements**:
1.  **Frosted Cards**: Background blur (backdrop-filter: blur(10px)) with a thin, semi-transparent white border.
2.  **Soft Shadows**: Large, diffuse colored shadows that match the content (glow effect).
3.  **Rounded Corners**: Generous border-radius (xl or 2xl) to enhance the organic feel.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
*   **Tactile Feel**: Buttons press down slightly when clicked.
*   **Smooth Flow**: Elements glide into place.
*   **Focus**: Background dims when a modal is active.

**Animation**:
*   **Parallax**: Subtle movement of background elements.
*   **Glass Shimmer**: A light shimmer effect on loading states.
*   **Float**: Gentle floating animation for empty states.

**Typography System**:
*   **Headings**: **Outfit** or **Plus Jakarta Sans** - Geometric and modern.
*   **Body**: **DM Sans** - Friendly and readable.
</idea>

### <idea>
**Design Movement**: **Neobrutalism (Functional Pop)**
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Raw & Honest**: Unapologetic structure, high contrast, and bold lines.
2.  **High Contrast**: Black borders, white backgrounds, and vibrant solid colors.
3.  **Systematic Layout**: Rigid grids that are made to be seen.
4.  **Playful Utility**: Serious tools don't have to be boring.

**Color Philosophy**:
*   **Stark White (#FFFFFF)**: The canvas.
*   **Pitch Black (#000000)**: For borders, text, and shadows.
*   **MOTI Blue (#2563EB)**: A solid, un-gradiented blue for primary actions.
*   **Functional Yellow/Green**: For highlighting and status, used in 100% opacity blocks.
*   *Intent*: To create a high-energy, high-clarity interface that prevents user fatigue through distinct visual separation.

**Layout Paradigm**:
*   **Grid Lines**: Visible borders separating all content areas.
*   **Sidebar as Toolbelt**: A distinct, separated column for tools.
*   **Brutal Headers**: Large, bold page titles that dominate the top section.

**Signature Elements**:
1.  **Hard Shadows**: Solid black shadows (box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px #000) for buttons and cards.
2.  **Thick Borders**: 2px or 3px solid black borders on everything.
3.  **Marquee/Ticker**: For announcements or live updates (optional).

**Interaction Philosophy**:
*   **Clicky**: Buttons have a distinct "down" state that removes the hard shadow.
*   **Snap**: Hover effects are instant, no fade.
*   **Direct**: No modals, just expanding sections or direct navigation.

**Animation**:
*   **Slide**: Panels slide in from the side with a hard stop.
*   **Blink**: Cursor blinking or status indicators.
*   **None**: Minimal animation to keep it snappy.

**Typography System**:
*   **Headings**: **Space Grotesk** or **Archivo Black** - Quirky but readable.
*   **Body**: **Public Sans** or **IBM Plex Sans** - utilitarian and crisp.
</idea>
